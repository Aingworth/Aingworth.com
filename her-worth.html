Why HER-Worth Exists

Women are not underperforming.
They are underrecognized.

37%

of domains AI regularly cites don't appear in traditional search top 10 results - the authority gap is structural, not personal

89%

of AI citations come from earned media - not paid ads, not follower counts, not social reach

The recognition economy is not gender-neutral. Women-led businesses and women executives face a compounding problem: the signals that AI systems use to determine authority have historically been easier to build for those who were already in the room. Thought leadership platforms, media coverage, speaking stages, and citation networks all carry structural bias that pre-dates the AI era.

That bias does not disappear when the search engine becomes an AI. In some ways it amplifies it. AI models learn from existing authority signals. If those signals underrepresent women's expertise, the model underrepresents women's expertise in its answers.

HER-Worth exists because the solution to a structural problem is a structural one. Not a mindset fix. Not a confidence workshop. Not a list of social media tips. A real, systematic approach to building the kind of authority that AI systems can find, interpret, and cite.

The same framework. Applied with full awareness of the additional context that women in business are navigating every single day.

The Framework

The Recognition Triangle,
applied to your reality.

The Recognition Triangle maps brand authority across three dimensions. All three need to work together for AI systems to recognize and cite your expertise consistently. HER-Worth applies this framework with full awareness of the structural challenges women face in building each dimension.

Clarity

How clearly your brand communicates what you stand for and who you serve. Women often know this internally with precision but have not yet made it legible to external systems.

Credibility

The external signals that verify your expertise: publications, citations, media, speaking, and structural presence. These have historically been harder to accumulate for women and we build them deliberately.

Earned Authority

The compounding reputation that comes from consistent, genuine expertise signals over time. Not manufactured. Not bought. Built in a way that holds up when AI systems evaluate it.

From Lucy

I built this because I needed it
and it didn't exist.

I have spent over a decade building growth systems for brands across the UK, USA, and Europe. I have been in the rooms, on the calls, and in the data. And I have watched women with genuinely better expertise, deeper knowledge, and stronger results get overlooked in favor of louder, less qualified voices.

The shift to AI-mediated discovery does not automatically fix that. AI systems cite what they can find, understand, and verify as authoritative. If the authority signals pointing to women's expertise are structurally weaker, the citations reflect that.

HER-Worth is my attempt to change that at the individual level while the structural problem is still being argued about at the systemic one. I am not waiting for the system to fix itself. Neither should you.

The framework in Become the Answer applies equally to everyone. But the conversation we have applying it to your specific situation is a different one when we both understand the full context of what you are navigating.
