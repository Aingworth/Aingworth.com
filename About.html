The Story

Built by someone who has done the work and still does.

Over a decade of working across digital strategy, SEO, CRO, paid media, and AI visibility strategy — not in one market or one industry, but across businesses in the USA, UK, and Europe, from luxury travel to SaaS to healthcare to e-commerce.

See how we work together

I did not build Aingworth because I had run out of interesting work. I built it because I kept seeing the same thing happen, and I could not unsee it.

Businesses with genuine capability, genuine insight, and a real offer — struggling to be found. Not because they were doing anything wrong. Because the systems designed to surface the best answer were surfacing the best optimized answer instead. And those are not always the same thing.

That gap bothered me. It still does. And it is exactly what Aingworth was built to close.

I have spent more than a decade diagnosing why growth stalls and designing the systems that get it moving again. Technical SEO for enterprises navigating complexity. CRO for brands with traffic they were not converting. Paid strategies for businesses spending without clarity on what it was actually buying. AI visibility and authority strategy for organizations that needed to be visible in a discovery landscape that had fundamentally shifted.

The clients spanned industries I never expected to go deep in — legal, finance, medical education, retail, hospitality, B2B SaaS. Each one taught me something the previous one had not. And the through-line in every engagement was the same: clarity before action, structure before scale, and systems that support people rather than exhaust them.

That is not a tagline. That is the only approach I have found that actually compounds over time rather than running out of steam the moment you stop pushing.

Aingworth is the full expression of that approach. Everything I have learned about what works, why it works, and what it requires of the people and organizations willing to build it properly.

What Drives This

Not just another consultant
with a polished story.

These are not values written for a website. They are the convictions that show up in every engagement, every conversation, and every decision about what Aingworth does and does not do.

01
The right brands deserve to be found.

There is a structural unfairness in how visibility works — where loudness and optimization have historically outperformed depth and genuine expertise. The shift to AI-mediated discovery is, in some ways, a correction. Aingworth exists to help the organizations ready to build that way.

02
Growth should support people, not pressure them.

I have seen what unsustainable growth pressure does to the people inside organizations and to the founders trying to hold everything together. Every system I design is built with that in mind. Clarity reduces pressure. Structure creates freedom. That is not a compromise on ambition. It is how ambition becomes durable.

03
Building this alongside you, not ahead of you.

Aingworth is not a finished brand offering a proven playbook from a distance. It is a brand being built in real time, using exactly the methods it recommends and documenting every step honestly. When I tell you the framework works, I am telling you from inside the implementation, not outside it.
