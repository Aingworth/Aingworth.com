The Shift

The old game was visibility.
The new game is recognition.

88%

of AI search citations go to pages outside the organic top 10 - Moz, 2026

86%

of AI citations come from sources brands can directly control - Yext, 2026

Search has changed in a way that most brands have not caught up with. When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI which consultant to hire, which book to read, or which brand to trust, they get an answer. Not a list of ten links. An answer.

Whether your brand is in that answer depends entirely on the authority signals you have built over time. Not your ad spend. Not your follower count. Not your position on page one of last year's search results.

The brands that built genuine, structured authority are the ones getting cited. The ones that chased metrics are invisible in the new system.

That is the gap Aingworth was built to close.
