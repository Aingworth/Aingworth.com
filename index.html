The Shift

The old game was visibility. The new game is recognition.

Search has changed in a way that most brands have not caught up with. When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI which consultant to hire, which book to read, or which brand to trust, they get an answer. Not a list of ten links. An answer.

Whether your brand is in that answer depends entirely on the authority signals you have built over time. Not your ad spend. Not your follower count. Not your position on page one of last year's search results.

The Answer

Recognition has three conditions.

The Recognition Triangle is the framework at the heart of Become the Answer. When all three conditions hold, you stop chasing attention and start being retrieved. When one fails, recognition decays.

Condition 01

Clarity

What you actually stand for. The unambiguous definition of who you are, what you do, and who it is for. If a machine cannot define you, it cannot retrieve you.

Condition 02

Credibility

What can be verified. The proof, track record and validation that let people and systems trust the claim you are making about yourself.

Condition 03

Earned Authority

What others say when you are not in the room. The citations, references and recognition that cannot be bought and cannot be faked.
