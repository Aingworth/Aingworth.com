Before You Write

Questions Lucy gets asked most often.

Do you work with clients outside South Africa?

Yes. Most consulting and coaching work happens virtually, so location is not a constraint. Lucy has worked with clients across the USA, UK, Europe, Australia, and Asia. All one-to-one work is conducted in English.

What is the typical starting point for a new consulting engagement?

Most engagements start with an AI Visibility Audit. It gives you and Lucy a shared, honest picture of where your brand currently stands before any strategy is built. From there the scope is defined collaboratively. See the services page for starting rates.

How far in advance do you need to book a speaking engagement?

For keynotes and conference appearances, four to six weeks is the minimum to allow for preparation and brief alignment. Corporate workshops can sometimes be arranged faster. Reach out as early as possible and Lucy will confirm availability.

Is the diagnostic really free?

Yes. The Recognition Diagnostic is completely free with no obligation. You get your pattern result immediately. Email capture at the end is optional and sends a more detailed breakdown to your inbox.

I want to get the book. Where can I buy it?

Become the Answer is available on Amazon in most major markets - USA, UK, Germany, Australia, and Canada. South Africa is not yet directly available but you can order via Amazon US or UK with international delivery. See the book page for all links.
